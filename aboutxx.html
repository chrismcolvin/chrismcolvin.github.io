About Me

I picked up photography as a hobby in 2009 after seeing my big brother with his fancy Nikon DSLR. Since then I have used photography to express the inspiration I find through the amazing places and moments surrounding me each and every day. In 2020 I began selling printed photos as a way to share that inspiration with others.

Web development is my day job. I find great joy in seeing so much abstraction come together to make something useful.

If you like my work, please consider supporting me by purchasing one of my photos.
