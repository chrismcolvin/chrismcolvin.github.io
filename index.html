The Fountain Pen Experiment

I’ve always been fascinated by pens, pencils, and other mark-making tools. However, I’ve never turned that interest into much more than a passing glance. Recently I finally decided to try something out that I’ve been looking into for some time: the fountain pen. Being a graphic-design student, I was certainly particular about my writing and drawing tools.
