The Fountain Pen Experiment /1

I’ve always been fascinated by pens, pencils, and other mark-making tools. However, I’ve never turned that interest into much more than a passing glance. Recently I finally decided to try something out that I’ve been looking into for some time: the fountain pen. Being a graphic-design student, I was certainly particular about my writing and drawing tools. I picked up a lead holder, and Micron pens, both of which I still use and love, but neither of which were ever particularly great for note taking or writing for long periods of time. Read more

About

After seeing my older brother take some really cool photos with his DSLR, I quickly developed a love and passion for photography, and have been pursuing that passion ever since. My love of design and architecture finds its way into many of the photos I take, and contributes to my ability to compose an image and notice the details.
